3. Key Insights & Findings
Based on the analysis of the data (Total Sales & Operating Profit), the following insights were discovered:

Top Performing Region: The West Region leads in profitability, contributing over $89.6M in operating profit.

Best-Selling Product: Men's Street Footwear is the most profitable product category, generating approximately $82.8M in profit.

Retailer Performance: West Gear and Foot Locker emerged as the top-tier retailers, significantly outperforming others in total sales volume.

Sales Channel Efficiency: In-store sales remain the dominant channel (approx. $127.6M in profit), though Online and Outlet channels show strong competitive growth.

Growth Trend: There was a massive surge in sales in 2021 compared to 2020, with Q3 2021 being the peak performance quarter, reaching over $80M in operating profit.

4. Dashboard Features
The interactive dashboard (as shown in the attached files) provides:

Dynamic Filtering: Users can filter results by Region, Retailer, and Sales Method.

Profitability Metrics: Real-time view of Operating Profit and Profit Margins.

Geographic Breakdown: Visual representation of sales across different states (e.g., California, New York, Florida).

Time-Series Analysis: Quarterly and Yearly profit trends to monitor business growth.

5. Conclusion & Recommendations
Expansion: Focus marketing efforts on the "West" and "Northeast" regions where profit margins are highest.

Product Focus: Increase inventory and variety in the "Street Footwear" and "Women's Apparel" categories.

Digital Transformation: While In-store is leading, the high volume of Online sales suggests a need for further optimization of the digital customer experience to increase the average invoice value.
